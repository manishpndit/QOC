Our Programs

We work holistically to ensure menstrual health is never a barrier to opportunity.

🌺

Sanitary Pad Distribution

We provide free, high-quality sanitary pads to underprivileged girls and women, ensuring they never miss school or work.

📘

Health Education & Awareness

Through interactive, culturally-sensitive sessions, we educate women and girls about menstrual health, breaking stigma.

🌟

Women Empowerment & Skills

From vocational training and skill development to mentorship, we create pathways to economic independence and confidence.
