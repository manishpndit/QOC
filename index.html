About Queens Of Change

Queens of Change Foundation is a women-led NGO dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged women and girls across India. We believe that menstrual health should never be a barrier to dignity, education, or opportunity. Founded on the principle that every woman deserves respect and support, we work directly in communities to provide sanitary pads, health education, and empowerment opportunities.
